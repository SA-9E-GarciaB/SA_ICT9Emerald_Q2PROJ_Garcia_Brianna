Mango Float

Ingredients


  • 2 large ripe mangoes (or about 3 cups of fresh mango slices)
  • 1 pack (200g) graham crackers (plain or honey graham)
  • 1 can (250ml) all-purpose cream (or heavy cream)
  • 1 can (300ml) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) evaporated milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • 1/2 cup (50g) chopped mangoes for garnish (optional)

Instructions


  • Peel the mangoes and slice them thinly, discarding the pit. Set aside some slices for garnishing the top of the dessert.
  • In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose cream, sweetened condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla extract (if using). Stir well until the mixture is smooth and fully combined.
  • In a 9x9-inch (23x23 cm) square or rectangular baking dish, place a layer of graham crackers at the bottom of the dish. You can break some crackers into pieces to fill in the gaps if needed. Spread a generous layer of the cream mixture over the graham crackers.
  • Add a layer of sliced mangoes on top of the cream mixture. Repeat the layers: another layer of graham crackers, followed by cream mixture, and a layer of mango slices. Keep layering until you run out of ingredients, making sure the top layer is cream and mangoes.
  • Cover the baking dish with plastic wrap or aluminum foil.
  • Refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or overnight if you want a firmer, more set dessert. This allows the graham crackers to soften and the flavors to meld together.
  • Before serving, garnish the top with more fresh mango slices or chopped mangoes for extra flavor and color.
  • Slice into squares and enjoy this refreshing, creamy dessert!
